## Introduction

Retail businesses rely heavily on understanding customer behavior, product demand, and sales trends to remain competitive. This project explores a fictional retail dataset from VIVA Fashion Company, transforming raw transactional data into a dynamic and interactive Excel dashboard.

The goal of this analysis was to uncover meaningful insights about customer purchasing patterns, product popularity, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction. Using Microsoft Excel’s analytical capabilities, the dataset was cleaned, organized, and summarized to build a dashboard that enables stakeholders to quickly understand business performance.

Through the use of Pivot Tables, charts, and calculated metrics, the dashboard provides a clear overview of sales activity over time, customer demographics, and operational efficiency.

Ultimately, this project demonstrates how Excel can be used as a powerful data analysis and visualization tool for retail decision-making.

## Dashboard Overview

The dashboard was designed to provide a comprehensive overview of VIVA Fashion Company’s sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

Rather than presenting raw data, the dashboard organizes key insights into clear visual sections that answer important business questions.

At the center of the dashboard are the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), which provide an immediate snapshot of overall performance.

### Page 1: Sales Summary – Portfolio-Level Performance
![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/Image%201.JPG)

#### 📌 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

- Total Orders – Total number of orders placed by customers

- Total Quantity – Total number of items sold

- Total Amount – Total revenue generated from sales

- Average Rating – Customer satisfaction based on product ratings

- Average Days to Deliver – Average shipping time from order to delivery

These KPIs give decision-makers a quick understanding of sales activity, operational efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

#### 📈 Sales Trend Analysis

One of the key objectives of the dashboard is to analyze sales performance over time.

Using Pivot Tables and charts, the dashboard tracks:

- Sales trends across the last 13 weeks

- Fluctuations in order volume

- Patterns that may indicate seasonal demand or promotional impacts

This allows stakeholders to monitor business momentum and identify potential growth opportunities or slow periods.

#### 🛍 Customer Purchasing Behavior

Understanding how customers shop is essential for retail strategy.

The dashboard explores:

- How customers prefer to buy

- How many items they typically purchase per order

- Popular product categories

These insights help businesses understand purchasing patterns and optimize inventory planning.

#### 👕 Product Popularity

The analysis identifies which products are performing best in terms of sales and quantity sold.

This helps answer key retail questions such as:

- Which products are driving the most revenue?

- Which items customers buy most frequently?

- Which product categories have the strongest demand?

These insights can guide marketing strategies and inventory management decisions.

#### 👥 Customer Demographics

The dashboard also analyzes customer demographics to better understand the company's audience.

Key insights include:

- Overall gender distribution of customers

- Geographical location of customers

This information helps businesses tailor marketing strategies and identify high-performing regions.

#### 🚚 Shipping & Delivery Performance

Operational efficiency plays an important role in customer experience.

The dashboard measures:

- Average days required to deliver orders

- Delivery performance across orders

This insight helps evaluate logistics efficiency and identify areas where shipping processes can be improved.

#### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction

Customer feedback is represented through average product ratings, giving insight into how satisfied customers are with their purchases.

By monitoring ratings, businesses can:

- Identify highly rated products

- Detect products that may require quality improvements

Track overall customer satisfaction levels



## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Microsoft Excel can be used as a powerful tool for transforming raw retail data into meaningful business insights. By leveraging Pivot Tables, charts, and calculated KPIs, the VIVA Fashion Company dataset was analyzed to reveal important patterns in sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product demand, and operational efficiency.

The dashboard provides a clear and structured view of the business by answering key questions related to sales trends, customer demographics, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction. Instead of relying on raw spreadsheets, stakeholders can quickly interpret the data through visual summaries and interactive elements
